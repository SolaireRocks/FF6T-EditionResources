Note: If you're using the custom Rage patch - https://pastebin.com/raw/NSsqpnZy - to custom sort your Rages they obviously won't reflect the order here.

Also if you miss any Rages simply complete Gau's quest with his father, talk to him, select the first option, and all Rage Formations will be open on the Veldt.

Feel free to also check the Enemy Formations tab to your left on the side bar and Ctrl + F search for any alternative locations for Rages if the location listed doesn't suit you.

Make sure to also check the magic html for any information on some of these unique attacks that have no information listed, too.

Make a note that whatever weapon(s) Gau has equipped are the weapons his Rage will use, including their elemental property. The weapon animations you see him using with a Rage are for show only, he's actually swinging his equipped ones.

Gau also gains the elemental/status resists and weaknesses of those he is Raging, so for coverage on that, check the Item Drops/Libra/Steal Data html and search the enemy you're interested in.

For an even more fun time with using Gau, use the Eidolon "Unicorn" if you want Gau to stop Raging without having to kill him to get him to stop.
