Note: If you're using the custom Rage patch (https://pastebin.com/raw/NSsqpnZy) to custom sort your Rages, they won't reflect the order here.

If you miss any Rages, complete Gau's quest with his father, talk to him, select the first option, and all Rage Formations will be open on the Veldt.

Check the Enemy Formations tab (on the original site's sidebar) and use Ctrl + F to search for alternative locations for Rages.

Refer to the magic HTML (presumably on the original site) for information on unique attacks.

Gau's equipped weapon(s) determine the Rage's weapon, including elemental properties. The animations are visual only; he's actually using his equipped weapons.

Gau gains the elemental/status resistances and weaknesses of the monster he's Raging. Check the Item Drops/Libra/Steal Data HTML (presumably on the original site) for details.

For more fun, use the Eidolon "Unicorn" to stop Gau from Raging without having to defeat him.
