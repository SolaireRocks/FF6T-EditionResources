Achievements

Conditions: Start the game.

  1. A new room has been added to the west most part of Narshe's Beginner's Hall that keeps track of your achievements.
  2. By reading the two books here you can gain hints on how to obtain each achievement.
  3. Currently there are 23 achievements to obtain.
  4. If you wish to check your current progress for each character's personal achievement, at least the ones requiring 500 uses or so, a new house north of Narshe in the WoR has an old man who will tell you.
  5. This new event uses a lot of unknown memory and due to this it may cause issues. If there's any serious problems I'll end up getting rid of it, but so far it's fine.
  6. To unlock something after completing the achievement, you must enter the room (so the game flags you having it. If you don't have a costume, but have completed the requirements, head here, then leave and it should work). Once unlocked it will remain even if the objectives are reset. Conditions: Clear the Magitek Research Facility. Locked out once you enter the WoR. Obtainable from the Bandit NPC in Tzen during the WoR.

  1. Complete the Magitek Research Facility event. After returning to Narshe and speaking with Banon, talk to Arvis to begin the battle. Once Banon and company leave for Vector, you can return to Narshe after the events in Thamasa to challenge them if you previously didn't do so. Outside the Narshe Item Shop are two old men NPCs walking around; one will prompt the Carbuncle fight, the other Catoblepas. Be warned, there is no asking if you're ready, so save prior.
  2. If you miss him, during the WoR he'll be availabe at the Bandit in Tzen once you obtain Seraphim.
  3. Defeat Carbuncle in battle.
  4. Upon winning you'll receive the Eidolon Carbuncle.

The Stolen Eidolon

Conditions: Clear the Magitek Research Facility. This will never expire.

  1. After clearing the Magitek Research Facility, speak to the thief in the northeast part of Tzen partially hidden in the trees.
  2. If you pay the 3000 Gil fee, you'll enter a battle against Seraphim.
  3. The old man NPC nearby stationary at the collapsing house will give a hint for this battle (use Sleep when she begins praying/text pops up in battle, text is not translated currently -Kain).

Lurking Underground

Conditions:

  • World of Balance: Once you can travel to the Sealed Cave. Ends when you depart for Thamasa.
  • World of Ruin: Once you can reopen the path to the Eidolon World.

  1. Examine the floor of the cave near the bottom of the long bridge near the end of the cave. (Suggested to look up a map where the hidden treasures of the Sealed Cave are. It's the northeast one.)
  2. A battle with the Gil Turtle will begin.
  3. Upon winning you'll obtain the "Gil Turtle's Shell" key item.

Extra Note: The "Gil Turtle's Shell" is necessary for the quest Seeking the Legendary Weapon.

Conditions: None. (Forced encounter.)

  1. Gilgamesh will appear near the end of the Sealed Cave once you activate the switch for the very long bridge to appear.

Destroy the Cave Demons

Conditions: Limited event. Complete before the end of the World of Balance.

  1. Speak to the barkeep in South Figaro and select the option "Defeat the Cave Demons" which upon accepting will teleport you to the event.
  2. Once you're moved to the South Figaro Cave defeat the stationary enemy to the north.
  3. Upon winning you'll be teleported back to the barkeep and receive 10 Potions, 5 Hi-Potions and 1 Phoenix Down for your work.

Extra Note: Open the treasure chests here as this is a separate map from South Figaro Cave and contain nice rewards.

Protect Mt. Koltz!

Conditions: Limited event. Finish before the end of the World of Balance.

  1. After the Imperial Banquet return and speak to the barkeep in South Figaro and select the "Protect Mt. Koltz!" option to be teleported to Mt. Koltz.
  2. Move through Mt. Koltz, avoiding falling rocks, and defeat the fixed enemy at the very end.
  3. Upon winning you'll be teleported back to the bar and receive a "Blood Lance" for your reward.

I'll Buy Anything!

Conditions: Limited event. Finish before the end of the World of Balance.

  1. Speak with the barkeep in Nikeah and select the option "I'll Buy Anything!".
  2. With the key item "Breathing Helmet" (Gau's Treasure) speak with the Nikeah Item Shop NPC and select to sell it for 25,000 Gil.

Extra Note:

  • The "Breathing Helmet" is automatically acquired during Sabin's scenario.
  • If you sell this for 25,000 Gil you will lose it.
  • If sold you may reacquire it in the World of Ruin at the Auction House.
  • Selling it does not stop you from diving into the Serpent Trench.

A Helping Hand

Conditions: Limited event. Finish before the end of the World of Balance.

  1. Speak to the barkeep in Kohlingen and select the "A Helping Hand" option.

New Game+

Conditions: Once you obtain the Falcon.

  1. After obtaining the Falcon, talking to the NPC behind the desk at the Narshe's Beginner's Hall (last option) will allow you to begin a New Game+.
  2. Note: Any unfinished bar side quests do NOT carry over; if you are aiming for the achievement, they must ALL be finished in 1 playthrough. If you missed one and are doing New Game+, ALL bar side quests have to be redone. Once you get the achievement, you can ignore any you don't care about on future playthroughs.
  3. The following carry over (levels are reset, so if planning to farm New Game+, don't do a lot of grinding until you're on the playthrough you intend to stay on):
  • Current items held.　※Key items will not carry over.
  • Magic spells (including % learned if not mastered).
  • Eidolons.
  • Blue Magic, Rages, and Dances.
  • Playtime.
  • Step count.
  • Steal success count.
  • Twin Fang count.
  • Successful uses of Runic.
  • Successful uses of Sketch.
  • Slots use total.
  • Snowstorm use total.
  • Successful uses of Mimic.
  • Unlocked achievements.
  • Tabs used (NOT Golden Apples or Soma Drops, however).

The following are added to "New Game+":

  • Monster formation encounters are reset.
  • Treasure chests are reset.
  • Trance time increase is reset （Humbaba event).
  • Terra loses Trance.
  • Deathgaze kill flag reset.
  • Reset Dried Meat flag.

The Return of the Narshe Residents

Conditions:During the World of Ruin after obtaining the Falcon. Speak to Banon in the Elder's House.

  1. Speak with Banon in the Elder's House and listen to him speak about the "Narshe Residents" (top option, speak to him twice).
  2. Track down and find all the residents, guards, and Returners around the world, then return to Narshe. The shops are opened once they've all been found.
  3. Once you've found everyone head to Arvis's House (where Terra wakes up at the start of the game) and speak to the old man NPC to receive the Eidolon Ragnarok.
  4. Shops unlocked will have basic supplies until you fully complete this quest. Check them once you obtain Ragnarok.

Location of Residents:

  • Thamasa: Returner at Leo's Grave (unlocks the Weapon Shop).
  • Dragon's Neck Coliseum: Returner at the Inn/Bar area (unlocks normal NPCs).
  • Kohlingen: Narshe Guard at the entrance (unlocks Narshe Guards and the Armor Shop).
  • Tzen: Female NPC inside the Inn (unlocks normal NPCs).
  • Tower of Fanatics: Elder NPC at the entrance (unlocks normal NPCs and the Inn).
  • Inside of Zone Eater: Male NPC in the area reached upon being knocked off a bridge (unlocks normal NPCs and the Item Shop).
  • South Figaro: Elder NPC in the bar's basement where you get the Cider/Wine (unlocks normal NPCs and the Relic Shop).

Extra Note:

  • Any locked doors are opened now, except the Cursed Shield house (still need Locke).
  • The previously locked treasure house (south of Arvis's house) is now open and the new Narshe Pub.

Seeking the Legendary Weapon

Conditions:After completing "The Return of the Narshe Residents" and receiving the Eidolon Ragnarok.

  1. After gathering all the residents of Narshe and receiving the Eidolon Ragnarok, speak to the Weapon Shop owner (backroom).
  2. Listen to the information about "Adamantite".
  3. Head to Gogo's Cave and in one of the rooms is a switch. Click it to battle Adamantoise and upon winning you'll obtain the "Adamantite" key item.
  4. Return to the Weapon Shop owner. You'll lose the "Adamantite" and receive the knight sword "Ragnarok".
  5. Once you receive "Ragnarok", speak to the owner again and you'll get a new quest to make an even stronger sword.
  6. After obtaining "Gil Turtle's Shell", "Omega's Armor", and "Shinryu's Fang", you'll receive the "Omega Weapon".

Extra Note:

  • For "Gil Turtle's Shell", see Lurking Underground.
  • For "Omega's Armor", defeat Omega in the Ancient Castle's Cave.
  • For "Shinryu's Fang", defeat Shinryu in the Sealed Cave treasure chest during the World of Ruin revisit.

The Sealed Legend

Conditions:

  • During the World of Ruin speak to Banon in Narshe and ask about the "Legendary Weapons".
  • Must have Cyan and Locke in your party's roster.

  1. Listen to the story about the "Legendary Weapons" from Banon in Narshe during the World of Ruin.
  2. In the northwest area of the Ancient Castle examine the sealed weapons behind their gates.
  3. Return to Banon who will mention scholars at Figaro Castle.
  4. In the Figaro Castle Library ask a scholar about the crystals.
  5. If you obtain the Crystals of Wind, Water, Fire, and Earth, each one will let you unlock 3 weapons.

Extra Note:

  • You can obtain the crystals in any order.
  • For each crystal once you've asked about them from the scholar, speak to Cyan, Locke, Setzer, and Gau in the airship to receive information on each. If they don't give hints, try putting them into their normal outfits first if they're in a costume, then talk again -Kain.

  3. Upon winning you'll receive the Water Crystal.
